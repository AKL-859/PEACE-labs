Welcome to the Power Electronics Research Group at Arizona State University, led by Professor Ayan Mallik. We specialize in wide band gap semiconductor devices and focus on advanced power electronics for electric vehicle charging, space applications, PV grid interfacing, data center power conversion, and optimization & reliability studies. Join us in shaping the future of sustainable and efficient power electronics.
